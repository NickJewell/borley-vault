## Summary

Price's complete chronological record of events at Borley, 1362–1939 — the master timeline of the case as presented in MHH.

## Key Dates

- **1362**: Edward III gave the Manor of Borley to the Benedictine monks of Christ Church, Canterbury
- **c.1500–1800**: Borley Manor in possession of the Waldegrave family
- **1862–1892**: Rev. Henry D. E. Bull, Rector; built the Rectory 1863; new wing added 1875–6
- **1886**: Mrs. E. Byford left Rectory as nursemaid due to "ghostly footsteps"
- **1892–1927**: Rev. Harry Foyster Bull, Rector
- **1900 (July 28)**: Four Misses Bull saw the nun in daylight on the lawn
- **1916–1920**: Coopers in cottage; "padding dog," nun sightings, coach-and-horses
- **1927 (June 9)**: Death of Rev. Harry Bull; **Autumn 1927**: Cartwright's four nun sightings
- **1928 (October 2)**: Rev. G. Eric Smith inducted
- **1929 (June 10–13)**: Borley first in press; Price's first visit; Wall's nun sighting; Blue Room séance
- **1930 (October 16)**: Foysters arrive; immediate phenomena
- **1931**: Peak Foyster period — worst outbreak February–June; Dom Richard's visits; June 8 worst single day
- **1932 (January 23–24)**: Marks Tey séance; manifestations mostly cease
- **1935 (October)**: Foysters move out
- **1936 (March 13)**: Rev. A. C. Henning inducted (combined Borley/Liston living)
- **1937 (May 19–June 2)**: Price begins tenancy; strange coat appears; base room established
- **1937 (May 25)**: Times advertisement for observers
- **1937–38**: Observer program; 48 investigators; phenomena throughout
- **1937 (October 23–31)**: Glanville table-tipping and first Planchette sessions; Mary Lairre named
- **1938 (March 27)**: "Sunex Amures" Planchette prediction of fire
- **1938 (May 9)**: Price moves out; golden ring found
- **1938 (August 22)**: Mrs. Wilson's insect; **November 1**: Gregson responds to Price's broadcast
- **1938 (December)**: Gregson takes possession; first dog disappears
- **1939 (February 27)**: Rectory destroyed by fire at midnight; apparitions during fire
- **1939 (March 16)**: Mayes hears phantom horses; **March 26**: Blue Room apparition at burnt window
- **1939 (June 21)**: "Psychic fête"; Price's last visit
