# Borley Postscript: Introduction

**Author:** [[peter-underwood|Peter Underwood]]
**Form:** Introduction to a collected volume of unpublished Borley material
**Slug:** `bps-intro`

## Summary

After nearly thirty years at Bentley in Hampshire, Underwood came across a quantity of unpublished material and illustrations pertaining to the Borley haunting. He had been involved with the case for nearly sixty years, had met "practically everyone who has had anything to do with the case," and had organised illustrated lectures at the London Savage Club, the Royal Photographic Society, various historic buildings and universities, and guided tours of Borley.

This volume is explicitly framed as "a postscript to all that has been published on Borley up to the present time" — containing material that never appeared in book form.

## Key Detail

The Savage Club connection: Harry Price was once a member of the Savage Club at 1 Whitehall Place, London SW1A 2HD. Underwood writes from the same address, suggesting his own membership there.
