# The Haunting of Borley Rectory — Chapter 12: The Return of Harry Price

**Source:** Sean O'Connor, *The Haunting of Borley Rectory* (c. 2022)
**Slug:** `hbrs-ch12`
**Time period:** 12 August – 14 October 1931

## Summary

The Marks Tey Circle séance; William Salter's visit; Price's return with Mollie Goldney.

### The Braithwaite Séance (12 August 1931)

John Braithwaite and Ernest Meads brought a medium called Johnson to the Rectory. The séance ran 4 hours. Contacted: a Native American guide; a girl who murdered her child; "Silly Billy" (a village idiot); a Harley Street doctor advising on Lionel's arthritis; Joe Miles (a drunken ex-publican who promised to stop causing disturbances); John Wesley; Cardinal Newman.

Marianne collapsed on the stairs during the séance, going into what Johnson called a trance. His verdict: Marianne was an advanced medium and the source of all phenomena. Her denial: "As if anyone in their five senses would trail the clean linen... over a dirty floor."

### William Salter's Visit (9 October 1931)

William Salter (SPR honorary secretary): physically resembled "an extremely intelligent and respectable Aberdeen terrier." Arrived with Sir George Whitehouse. Tried to dissuade Lionel from using Price; tried to get Ethel Bull to block the visit. Ethel refused — "He had a cheek to ask." Salter warned Lionel he would regret it.

### Price's Return (13–14 October 1931)

Price arrived with Mollie Goldney, Clarice Richards, May Walker, and chauffeur James Ballantyne. Marianne's description of Price: "He had pointed ears, a balding head with a high forehead and eyes which were startling. They were not polite eyes."

The wine-to-ink trick: Marianne poured Burgundy and it turned jet black; the Sauternes smelled like eau de cologne. Mollie Goldney "roared with laughter at what was obviously a conjuring trick."

Ballantyne the chauffeur saw a "grisly black hand" move slowly up and down in the hallway — remained completely unperturbed. "Just part of the business." 

A door locked itself with Marianne inside the Blue Room; Lionel prayed with the Curé d'Ars relic; the door unlocked.

Next morning Price told Lionel there was "not the slightest doubt" Mrs Foyster was responsible. He proposed confining the entire household to one room for a test night. Marianne accepted defiantly. The bells rang and Price concluded Adelaide had rung them from her sealed room. As visitors left, Marianne shouted after Price: "Go and never come back!"
