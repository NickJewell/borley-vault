## Summary

Alphabetical list of approximately 90 named witnesses to phenomena or alleged phenomena at Borley Rectory, as referenced in the monograph. A reference document.

## Key Inclusions

The list includes all official observers who signed the Declaration Form, all resident witnesses (Bull family, Smiths, Foysters, Gregson), independent witnesses (Coopers, Cartwright, Hardy, Mayes, Byford), and investigators (Price himself, Glanville, Henning).

Notable entries: Adelaide Foyster (child witness); Lucie Kaye (Price's secretary); Walter Bull; Katie (Foysters' maid); James Ballantyne (chauffeur); Rosemary Williams; C.G. Browne; various BBC personnel; Oxford and Cambridge academics.

Total named witnesses: approximately 90 individuals (Price's "100 witnesses" count in the text includes unnamed villagers and persons).

## Significance

Reference document. Confirms the scale of the witness base claimed by Price. See [[the-observer-program]], [[mhh-app-d]].
