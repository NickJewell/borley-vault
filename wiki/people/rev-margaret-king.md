# Rev. Margaret King

Rector of Borley (as of 2022), serving fourteen parishes over a district of 80 square miles; the Church of England minister who put Sean O'Connor in touch with local villagers for his research.

## Background

Margaret King took over as rector of Borley in 2020, inheriting a multi-parish ministry that takes in fourteen churches. At Borley, approximately twenty parishioners attend the Sunday service, which is held only once every two months due to the scale of her pastoral responsibilities.

## Role with the Book

O'Connor discovered that Margaret King was an avid fan of *The Archers*, which he had previously worked on for BBC Radio. He used this connection to persuade her to put him in touch with local villagers willing to speak about Borley and its history — in particular [[michael-stebbing|Michael Stebbing]].

She continues to receive two or three requests a month from "out-and-out cranks" who wish to spend a night in the church.
