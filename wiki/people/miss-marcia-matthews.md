# Miss Marcia Matthews

Companion of [[jon-simons|Jon Simons]] on a visit to [[borley-church|Borley Church]] on **4 February 1970**. Both she and Simons heard footsteps coming up the church path while they were inside; no one entered or was found in the churchyard on investigation [1].

This is one of a repeated pattern of auditory phenomena at the church path recorded by multiple unrelated witnesses across different decades — a pattern noted by researchers as significant.

The sources record no further biographical details, quotes, or contradictions regarding Miss Marcia Matthews beyond this single incident [1].
