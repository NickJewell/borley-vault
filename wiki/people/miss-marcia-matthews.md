# Miss Marcia Matthews

Companion of [[jon-simons|Jon Simons]] on a visit to [[borley-church|Borley Church]] on 4 February 1970. Both she and Simons heard footsteps coming up the church path while they were inside; no one entered or was found in the churchyard on investigation. No further details appear in *Ghosts of Borley*.
