# John Snagge

BBC broadcaster and an official observer during [[harry-price|Harry Price]]'s tenancy of [[borley-rectory|Borley Rectory]] (1937–38). Snagge was at this time a prominent BBC announcer and sports commentator — his name on Price's observer list, alongside [[sj-de-lotbiniere|S. J. de Lotbinière]] and others from the BBC, reflects the wide public-interest dimension of the Borley investigation.

No specific phenomena are recorded against Snagge's visits in *Ghosts of Borley*. He appears in the list of observers alongside Lotbinière, Major H. Douglas-Home, Lloyd Williams (BBC Educational Department director), and the television engineer M. Savage.
