# Colonel Boothby

Husband of [[constance-brackenbury|Constance Brackenbury]], [[harry-bull|Harry Bull]]'s stepdaughter by his wife [[ivy-brackenbury|Ivy Brackenbury]]. Constance married Boothby at eighteen; the Colonel was described as "considerably older than herself." He subsequently died at Cheltenham. Following his death, Constance moved to South Africa, where she died in 1970.

No further biographical details about Colonel Boothby appear in *Ghosts of Borley*.
