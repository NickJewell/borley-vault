# Peter Eton

BBC. Visited the Borley Rectory site in June 1947, two days before the BBC's broadcast "The Haunted Rectory" (transmitted 29 June 1947), accompanied by BBC colleague [[alan-burgess|Alan Burgess]].

During their visit, while Eton stood on a concrete slab at one side of the ruins and Burgess faced him on the opposite side, a number of short, sharp raps were heard coming from the former cellars. Eton asked the raps to stop and start again — and they complied. This occurred in broad daylight. A recording of the raps was made and included in the broadcast.
