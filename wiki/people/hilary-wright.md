# Hilary Wright

A former Borley resident, living in a small Suffolk town at the time of interview (1995). A former nurse. Daughter of [[marie-poore]]. Interviewed twice by Babbs and Mathias in 1995.

## Nun Sighting — Autumn 1971

Being driven home at about 2am from a social function, the car approached Borley from Rodbridge Corner. The night was clear and moonlit with good visibility. Both Mrs Wright and her companion saw a figure of a nun standing beyond the end of Rodbridge bridge, to their left, slightly before the turning up to Borley.

She could not discern facial features but the figure was:
- Dressed in **long grey robes**
- Either notably tall, or apparently "**standing upon high ground which is no longer there**"
- Associated with a feeling she described as "**bad vibes**" — unpleasant without being violent

She is a former nurse and "seemed to us to be a reliable witness."

## BBC Television — August 1974

Living at Pond Cottage near the rectory site, she appeared in a BBC television programme hosted by [[peter-underwood]] in August 1974, along with her mother [[marie-poore]], [[ursula-pennell]], and Canon Pennell. Participants received £10 each.

## Phantom Cat — 1974

During the summer of 1974, being driven through Borley at dusk, a large cat dashed out into the road at a specific spot and then vanished. The driver did not see the animal. [[marie-poore]] saw the same phenomenon at the same location in the same year on a different occasion.
