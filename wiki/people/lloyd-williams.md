# Lloyd Williams (and Mrs. Lloyd Williams)

Lloyd Williams was "a Director of the B.B.C. Educational Department." He and his wife visited Borley Rectory on February 18, 1938 as part of a BBC party that included [[c-gordon-glover|Mr. and Mrs. C. Gordon Glover]]. Mrs. Lloyd Williams is the key witness of the visit: she reported seeing a dark figure moving along the [[nuns-walk]] — the most specific BBC-period sighting of the nun.

## Mrs. Lloyd Williams's Nun Sighting

At approximately 6:10 p.m., the party was positioned in the large summer-house watching the Nun's Walk. Glover and his wife watched the open section of the Walk; Mr. and Mrs. Lloyd Williams kept their eyes on the line of trees at the Rectory end.

Mrs. Lloyd Williams "suddenly tensed" and said nothing for ten seconds. She then declared that she had "distinctly seen 'a round, dark object.'" It had appeared to move from the tree closest to the Rectory to the central fir-tree, where it vanished. She "had not seen it actually progress, but it had somehow covered the distance between the two trees." The light was "deep twilight."

Glover notes that the other three members of the party immediately walked across the lawn and in front of the trees to test their visibility from the summer-house — Mrs. Lloyd Williams confirmed she could just see them, clothed in light-coloured coats.

**Her conviction was unshaken:** "One witness only whose faith that she saw something is not to be shaken."

[[harry-price]] places this alongside the V. C. Wall sighting from the same summer-house in June 1929 as comparable experiences from the same vantage point nearly a decade apart.

## Also at the Visit

**Mrs. Lloyd Williams** additionally reported hearing "six quick, young footsteps" in the passage outside the scullery during an 11 p.m. house tour — though the others present heard nothing, and Glover notes in his report that "it is our opinion that these steps were imagined."

**Lloyd Williams himself** is listed in Appendix D as a witness to "Clicks or Cracks" and "Knocks, Bumps, Thuds" (the collective experiences at 12:30 a.m. in the Blue Room) but has no individual paranormal experience recorded.
