# Gordon Cartwright

Diesel engineer from Manchester; husband of [[adelaide-foyster|Adelaide Foyster Cartwright]] (formerly Barbara Tower), whom she married around 1950.

## Background

Gordon was a diesel engineer based in Manchester. [[adelaide-foyster|Adelaide]] had started training as a nurse in a Manchester hospital in 1944 after leaving the care of the Foyster family. She met and married Gordon in Manchester around 1950.

Little detail is provided about Gordon himself beyond his trade and city. His primary significance is as Adelaide's husband, the man who gave her the stable married life she had been denied at [[borley-rectory|Borley Rectory]].

## Significance

When [[wallace-tower|Wallace Tower]] was tracking down his sister in the early 1970s, it was as **Adelaide Cartwright** in Manchester that she was eventually found — through the research of [[alan-roper|Alan Roper]]. She received Wallace's phone call as Mrs Cartwright in November 1974, and the Tower family reunion took place under her married name.

When the Foyster Bible passed to [[vincent-oneil|Vince O'Neil]] after Marianne's death, it was a letter from "Adelaide Cartwright, formerly Adelaide Foyster" that requested its return.
