# James Ballantyne

[[harry-price|Harry Price]]'s chauffeur, who drove the party to [[borley-rectory|Borley Rectory]] on 13 October 1931 for Price's first visit during the [[lionel-foyster|Foyster]] incumbency. The party comprised Price, [[mrs-henry-richards|Mrs Henry Richards]], [[kathleen-goldney|Mrs K. M. Goldney]], [[miss-may-walker|Miss May Walker]], and Ballantyne, all of whom stayed at the Bull Hotel in [[long-melford|Long Melford]] during the visit.

Ballantyne was present as an observer for the all-night sitting during which the entire household was confined to the kitchen after the wine-into-ink and bell-ringing incidents. He is named once and no further details about him appear in *Ghosts of Borley*.
