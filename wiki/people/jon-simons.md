# Jon Simons

Of Higham Park, London, with a long-standing interest in the ghosts and folklore of Essex. Despite visiting Borley many times without having any supernormal experiences on his initial trips, Simons had one recorded incident on **4 February 1970** (a Saturday, according to the sources — though this date historically fell on a Wednesday [1]). He and [[miss-marcia-matthews|Miss Marcia Matthews]] were inside [[borley-church|Borley Church]] reading entries in the Visitors' Book when they heard footsteps coming up the outside path [1, 2]. No one entered the church; there was no one in the porch or anywhere in the churchyard when they looked [1, 2].

His experience was one of many post-fire reports of auditory phenomena at the church, despite the absence of any Rectory and no formal investigation under way.
