# Roland Winder

Roland Winder is named in the preface to the SPR's 1956 *Haunting of Borley Rectory* report as one of those who provided "information and advice" to the authors. He is listed among a group of people thanked for their assistance, including Canon H. Lawton, Herbert E. Pratt, Lord Charles Hope, Major Douglas-Home, Charles Sutton, Cynthia Thompson, Roger Glanville, Helen Carter (née Glanville), the late Lady Whitehouse, Douglas Craggs, and Mrs E. M. Wildgoose.

## Research Notes

*Sourced via NotebookLM corpus research, 2026-05-30.*

**Roland Winder** appears very briefly in the Borley Rectory sources, featuring solely in the acknowledgments of the highly critical 1956 Society for Psychical Research (SPR) publication, *The Haunting of Borley Rectory* (the *Borley Report*) [1, 2]. 

In the preface of their report, authors Eric J. Dingwall, Kathleen M. Goldney, and Trevor H. Hall explicitly list Mr. Roland Winder among the "many others to whom we are much indebted for information and advice" during the preparation of their investigation into Harry Price and the Borley hauntings [2].
