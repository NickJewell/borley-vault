# H.F. Frost

Member of the [[marks-tey-spiritualist-circle|Marks Tey Spiritualist Circle]] who travelled to [[borley-rectory|Borley Rectory]] with [[george-warren|George Warren]] and [[captain-deane|Captain Deane]] on the night of 22 January 1932, and participated in the January 1932 séance.

## Role

Frost arrived with Warren and Captain Deane unexpectedly after midnight on 22 January 1932, informing [[lionel-foyster|Lionel Foyster]] that the "helpers on the other side" had warned that [[marianne-foyster|Marianne]]'s spirit protectors could not hold out much longer. He returned the following night for the full séance conducted by medium [[guy-lestrange|Guy L'Estrange]].

Beyond his presence as one of the Circle's representatives at Borley, Frost's individual role is not described in detail in the sources.
