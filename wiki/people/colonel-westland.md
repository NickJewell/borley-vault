# Colonel Westland

An official observer during [[harry-price|Harry Price]]'s tenancy of [[borley-rectory|Borley Rectory]] (1937–38). He discovered a piece of wood on the stairs and a round blue box containing pins which had not been reported before. During a subsequent visit, he noted that the blue box had moved half an inch out of the chalked circle Price used to mark objects' positions — one of the more controlled instances of unexplained object displacement reported by the observer programme.

No further biographical details about Westland are given in *Ghosts of Borley*.
