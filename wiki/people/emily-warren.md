# Emily Warren

Wife of [[george-warren|George Warren]] and active participant in the [[marks-tey-spiritualist-circle|Marks Tey Spiritualist Circle]]'s January 1932 séance at [[borley-rectory|Borley Rectory]].

## Role

Emily accompanied the Marks Tey group to Borley for the overnight séance on 23 January 1932. She brought the **planchette** — a small, heart-shaped wooden board with castors for automatic writing — which was used that night. During the sitting, it was Emily who spoke directly to the spirit of the Borley nun (channelled through medium [[guy-lestrange|Guy L'Estrange]]):

> 'No, dear child. Go with the lady who stands behind you and is helping you now. She will take you to a place of rest and beauty. Your terrible sufferings are over. Put them out of your mind. You are now going to progress in your new life and be very, very happy.'

She also brought **a bowl of primroses** to the table when the spirit asked for spring flowers. L'Estrange buried his face in them and smiled.
