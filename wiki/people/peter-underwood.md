# Peter Underwood (PU)

Co-author (with [[paul-tabori]]) of *Ghosts of Borley* (1973). Identified in the book as "PU."

## Role

Spent many years gathering and analysing the "vast material" on the Borley case. Conducted first-hand interviews with many of the surviving witnesses, including [[ethel-bull]], Mr J. Harley, and L. A. Foyster's brother. His personal interviews with ageing witnesses are a primary source for accounts of the Bull period.

## Declared Position

Believes some of the Borley phenomena were genuine. Rejects the indictment against [[harry-price]] made by the 1956 SPR report.
