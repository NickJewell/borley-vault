# Captain Ben Martindale

From Ockenham. One of the three who spent the night at Borley on Saturday 8 April 1961 alongside [[st-john-saunders|St John Saunders]] and [[karl-barton|Karl Barton, BSc]]. All three reported hearing footsteps and seeing lights in the churchyard and over the Rectory site for which they could find no explanation. During the vigil, their torches and car headlights failed simultaneously. No further details appear in *Ghosts of Borley*.
