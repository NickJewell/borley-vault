# Captain Ben Martindale

From Ockenham. One of three investigators who spent the night at Borley on Saturday 8 April 1961, alongside [[st-john-saunders|St John Saunders]] (a publisher from Chelmsford) and [[karl-barton|Karl Barton, BSc]] (a civil engineer) [1].

## The April 1961 Visit

Despite the Rectory having been completely demolished by this time, the party reported experiencing paranormal activity during their vigil. All three reported hearing **"unaccountable footsteps"** and seeing **"bright lights"** hovering over the former Rectory site for which they could find no explanation [1]. During the vigil, their torches and car headlights also failed simultaneously.

No further biographical details about Captain Ben Martindale appear in the sources beyond this single documented investigation.
