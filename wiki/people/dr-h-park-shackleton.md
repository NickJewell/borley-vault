# Dr H. Park Shackleton

Dr H. Park Shackleton is named in the preface to the SPR's 1956 *Haunting of Borley Rectory* report as one of the people who provided "information and advice" that assisted the investigation. He is mentioned alongside [[dr-john-park-shackleton|Dr John Park Shackleton]], suggesting the two are related (likely father and son).

Nothing further about his role or background is specified in the report.

His sole appearance in the material relates to the highly critical re-examination of the Borley case conducted by the Society for Psychical Research (SPR) following Harry Price's death. In the preface to their 1956 publication, *The Haunting of Borley Rectory* (commonly known as the *Borley Report*), authors Dr. Eric J. Dingwall, Kathleen M. Goldney, and Trevor H. Hall explicitly list Dr. H. Park Shackleton—alongside Dr. John Park Shackleton—as someone to whom they were **"much indebted for information and advice"** while compiling their case [1].
