# Dr H. Park Shackleton

Dr H. Park Shackleton is named in the preface to the SPR's 1956 *Haunting of Borley Rectory* report as one of the people who provided "information and advice" that assisted the investigation. He is mentioned alongside [[dr-john-park-shackleton|Dr John Park Shackleton]], suggesting the two are related (likely father and son).

In the preface to their 1956 publication, authors [[eric-dingwall|Dr. Eric J. Dingwall]], [[kathleen-goldney|Kathleen M. Goldney]], and [[trevor-hall|Trevor H. Hall]] explicitly list Dr H. Park Shackleton among a select group of individuals to whom they were "much indebted for information and advice" while compiling their case against [[harry-price|Harry Price]] [1]. The sources do not specify the exact nature of the information or advice he provided.
