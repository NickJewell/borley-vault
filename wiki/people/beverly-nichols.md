# Beverly Nichols

British writer. Nichols interviewed [[jose-simmonds|Terrence Bacon]] about his claimed sightings of [[the-nun|the Nun]] at [[rectory-cottage|Borley Priory cottage]], where the Bacon family lived from 1951. Terry told Nichols that he had seen the nun-like form disappear at a gap in the hedge, and remarked — with characteristic directness — that he "thought it funny she didn't tear her dress on the brambles!"

No further connection between Nichols and the Borley case is recorded in *Ghosts of Borley*.
