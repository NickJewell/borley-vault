# Sub-Lt Alastair McIver, RN

One of [[miss-susanna-dudley|Miss Susanna Dudley]]'s companions on a visit to [[borley-church|Borley Church]] on 20 February 1949. On the path to the church, McIver was one of three who encountered the unexplained intense smell that [[sub-lt-crossley|Sub-Lt Jonathan Crossley]] could not detect at all. McIver said it was "the most disgusting thing he had ever smelt."
