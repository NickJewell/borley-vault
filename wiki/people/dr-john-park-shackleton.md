# Dr John Park Shackleton

Dr John Park Shackleton is named in the preface to the SPR's 1956 *Haunting of Borley Rectory* report as one of those who provided "information and advice" to the investigators. He is mentioned alongside [[dr-h-park-shackleton|Dr H. Park Shackleton]], suggesting a family relationship (likely father and son).

Nothing further about his role is specified in the report.

**Contribution to the SPR *Borley Report***
Dr. John Park Shackleton is mentioned exclusively in the preface to the 1956 publication *The Haunting of Borley Rectory* (commonly known as the *Borley Report*), authored by Society for Psychical Research (SPR) investigators Dr. Eric J. Dingwall, Kathleen M. Goldney, and Trevor H. Hall [1, 2]. The authors explicitly list him—directly alongside Dr. H. Park Shackleton—among a select group of individuals to whom they were **"much indebted for information and advice"** while compiling their case against Harry Price [1]. 

The available sources do not record any lesser-known biographical details, contradictions, or specific events regarding his life, nor do they specify the exact nature of the information or advice he provided to the investigators to assist with their report [1].
