# Dr John Park Shackleton

Dr John Park Shackleton is named in the preface to the SPR's 1956 *Haunting of Borley Rectory* report as one of those who provided "information and advice" to the investigators. He is mentioned alongside [[dr-h-park-shackleton|Dr H. Park Shackleton]], suggesting a family relationship (likely father and son).

In the preface to their 1956 publication, authors [[eric-dingwall|Dr. Eric J. Dingwall]], [[kathleen-goldney|Kathleen M. Goldney]], and [[trevor-hall|Trevor H. Hall]] explicitly list Dr John Park Shackleton — directly alongside Dr H. Park Shackleton — among a select group of individuals to whom they were "much indebted for information and advice" while compiling their case against [[harry-price|Harry Price]] [1, 2]. The available sources do not specify the exact nature of the information or advice he provided, nor do they record any further biographical details.
