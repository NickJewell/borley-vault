## How to retry failed pages

The enricher script processes the `queued[]` list by `current_index`. To retry
failed pages, temporarily prepend them to the front of the queue in the JSON:

```python
import json
d = json.load(open('wiki/.nlm-enrichment-progress.json'))
# Move failed pages back into the queue at current position
d['queued'][d['current_index']:d['current_index']] = d['failed']
d['failed'] = []
json.dump(d, open('wiki/.nlm-enrichment-progress.json', 'w'), indent=2)
```

Then run: `python3 borley/.nlm-enricher.py 26`
